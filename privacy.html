Privacy Policy

Last updated: 11 April 2026

1. Introduction

Prudentia Digital (Pty) Ltd ("we", "us", "our") is committed to protecting your personal information in accordance with the Protection of Personal Information Act, 2013 (POPIA). This Privacy Policy explains what information we collect, how we use it, and your rights regarding your personal data.

By using our website at prudentiadigital.co.za or contacting us through any means, you acknowledge that you have read and understood this policy.

2. Information We Collect

We collect the following categories of personal information:

  • Contact form submissions: name, email address, company name, and message content when you use our contact form.
  • Website analytics: anonymised page views and interaction data (via Cloudflare Web Analytics, which does not use cookies or collect personal identifiers).
  • Communications: records of emails or other correspondence you initiate with us.

We do not collect sensitive personal information (as defined under POPIA) unless specifically required and with your explicit consent.

3. How We Use Your Information

We use your personal information to:

  • Respond to inquiries and provide our consulting services;
  • Communicate with you about projects and engagements;
  • Improve our website and service offering;
  • Comply with legal and regulatory obligations.

4. Legal Basis for Processing (POPIA)

We process your personal information on the following grounds:

  • Consent: When you submit our contact form, you consent to us processing that information to respond to your inquiry.
  • Legitimate interest: We use anonymised analytics data to understand how our website is used and improve it — this does not involve personal identifiers.
  • Legal obligation: Where required by South African law, including tax and financial regulations.

5. Data Sharing

We do not sell, rent, or trade your personal information. We may share your data with the following trusted sub-processors solely to deliver our services:

  • Cloudflare: Website hosting and DNS (Cloudflare Workers / Pages). Data processed in accordance with Cloudflare's Privacy Policy.
  • Email service providers: Used to deliver responses to your inquiries. We select providers that comply with applicable data protection requirements.

We will disclose personal information if required by law, court order, or to protect the rights and safety of Prudentia Digital and others.

6. Your Rights Under POPIA

As a data subject under POPIA, you have the right to:

  • Access: Request a copy of the personal information we hold about you.
  • Correction: Request correction of inaccurate or incomplete personal information.
  • Deletion: Request deletion of your personal information, subject to applicable legal retention requirements.
  • Objection: Object to the processing of your personal information where we rely on legitimate interest.
  • Data portability: Request your personal information in a structured, machine-readable format.
  • Withdraw consent: Where processing is based on consent, withdraw it at any time without affecting the lawfulness of prior processing.

To exercise any of these rights, contact our Information Officer at masekolt@prudentiadigital.co.za. We will respond within 30 days.

7. Data Retention

We retain personal information for as long as necessary to fulfil the purposes for which it was collected. Contact form submissions and inquiry data are retained for a maximum of 2 years from the date of last contact, after which they are securely deleted. Legal and financial records are retained in accordance with applicable South African law.

8. Security

We implement appropriate technical and organisational measures to protect your personal information, including:

  • HTTPS encryption for all data transmitted to and from our website;
  • Access controls limiting who can access personal data;
  • Regular review of our data processing practices.

While we take reasonable steps to protect your information, no internet transmission is 100% secure. If you become aware of any security vulnerability, please notify us immediately.

9. Cookies

Our website uses minimal cookies. Cloudflare may set functional cookies for security and performance purposes. We use Cloudflare Web Analytics, which is cookieless and privacy-preserving — it does not track individual visitors or collect personal identifiers.

You can manage cookie preferences through your browser settings. Disabling cookies may affect website functionality.

10. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. The "Last updated" date at the top of this page will reflect any changes. We encourage you to review this policy periodically. Continued use of our website after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.

11. Contact

Information Officer: Prudentia Digital (Pty) Ltd
Email: masekolt@prudentiadigital.co.za

If you believe your rights under POPIA have been infringed, you have the right to lodge a complaint with the Information Regulator of South Africa:

  • Website: www.justice.gov.za/inforeg/
  • Email: inforeg@justice.gov.za
