The Solution

Peach Payments — the unique intersection.

Of the 21 gateways Capitec officially supports for Capitec Pay, only one is also in Wix's curated payment-provider catalogue for South Africa: Peach Payments. PayFast, Stitch, Ozow and PayU all support Capitec Pay — but none have a Wix connector. Yoco has a Wix connector but doesn't surface "Capitec Pay" by name. Peach is the only path that wins on every constraint.

Why this works

Every constraint, satisfied.

  • "Capitec Pay" appears by name at your checkout — not a generic "EFT" or card option. Customers see the brand they trust.
  • No Velo, no code. All configuration happens in two web dashboards: Wix's and Peach Payments'. You can drive it yourself after delivery.
  • Wix officially supports Peach Payments. Listed on Wix's South Africa providers page — no workarounds, no warranty risk.
  • Capitec officially supports Peach Payments. Named on Capitec's own Capitec Pay provider list.
  • Verbatim per-transaction fee. 1.50% + R1.50 per Capitec Pay transaction (volume-based). No setup fee. No monthly fee under R500K/mo volume.
  • POPIA-friendly by design. Open-banking flow means no card numbers ever touch your store.
  • Same gateway, more methods. Once Peach is connected, you also unlock cards, Ozow, Apple Pay and EFT without further work.

Operational Reality · NEW in Rev 1

What this means day-to-day.

Two things about the Wix↔Peach integration we want to set straight up-front, sourced verbatim from Peach Payments' own developer documentation:

Refunds run from Peach, not Wix

Verbatim from Peach docs: "Merchants must use the Peach Payments Dashboard to log refunds as Peach does not support full or partial refunds through the Wix platform."

Operational impact: you log refunds in the Peach BIP dashboard (full or partial, supported). We document the exact workflow in your handover.

Order-status sync is manual

Verbatim from Peach docs: "Peach Payments does not support webhooks for Wix, meaning there is no automatic synchronization of order status updates. Merchants may need to manually manage order statuses and transactions."

Operational impact: you reconcile Wix orders against Peach transactions end-of-day. We supply you with a simple SOP for this.

Why we surface this: an earlier draft of this proposal stated refunds flow through the Wix dashboard and order sync was automatic. Both were wrong. We checked Peach's own docs in the second-pass review and corrected them here.
